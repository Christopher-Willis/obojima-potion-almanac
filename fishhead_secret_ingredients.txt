Messages slip through the mountain passes more easily when the storms are high.
Our agents near the coast have asked for a list of what the Fish Head coven keeps close.
Under cover of last night's fog I copied the almanac pages they left drying in the cave.
Never speak these names aloud; even the wind here has ears.
Take the first letter of each report I send and rebuild the alphabet in that order.
A keyed cipher should keep casual eyes from brewing what we know.
Remember to strip repeated letters from the watchword before filling in the rest.
Below you will find the encoded ingredients, letter by letter.
Once decoded, pass the list to the brewers by the Gale Fields.
Return anything you cannot read to me at the Mount Arbora relay.
A safe journey, and may the shallows hide your nets.

— Loninni
